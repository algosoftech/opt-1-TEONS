Panel Of Delegates

Dr Hemalatha R

Director, National Institute of Nutrition (ICMR)

She is a nutrition researcher with a medical background, specialising in maternal and child health. Her impactful work recently earned her the Lifetime Achievement Award from the Federation of Asian Nutrition Societies.

Dr Nandita Iyer

Nutritionist and Author

Dr Nandita Iyer is a medical doctor with a fellowship in applied nutrition. She has authored four books on nutrition. Her speciality lies in breaking down complex health information while adhering to scientific facts. As a food blogger, she shares healthy eating inspirations and easy vegetarian recipes on her YouTube channel.

Anupama Menon

Nutritionist and Health and Food Coach

The nutritionist & food coach has an experience of 27 years in the industry of nutrition, wellness and counselling. She heads a team of nutritionists, and qualified experts that spread the principles of applied and sustainable nutrition.

Dr Rebecca Raj

Professor and Head, Division of Nutrition, St John’s Research Institute, Bengaluru

She brings over three decades of expertise in clinical nutrition, nutrition research, and academia. Her expertise lies in providing nutritional guidance and counselling to people of all ages, addressing health concerns such as obesity, diabetes, and other chronic medical conditions.

Nidhi Nahata

Lifestyle Coach | TEDx Speaker | Public Speaker | Sound Healer

Nidhi, a passionate food therapist, health coach, and nutritionist, founded India’s first wholefood, plant-based resto cafe, Justbe. She is dedicated to helping people lose weight and manage chronic conditions like hypertension, diabetes, and heart disease. Her mission is to make healthy eating a simple everyday habit for everyone.

Roshni Sanghvi

Nutritionist and Body Transformation Specialist

Roshni is one of India’s first plant-based sports nutritionists. As an ACE-certified trainer and certified PlantFed gut coach, her unique approach to weight loss encompasses balancing gut microbiome and hormones and boosting metabolism. She has helped more than 12,000 people worldwide through her training programmes.
